Tacos

Description

Delicious homemade tacos with a variety of fresh ingredients and flavors. Perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Taco shells
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook meat in a skillet over medium heat until browned.
  2. Warm taco shells in the oven.
  3. Chop tomatoes, lettuce, and onion.
  4. Assemble tacos with meat, vegetables, cheese, and your favorite toppings.
