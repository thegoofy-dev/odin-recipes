Pizza

Description

Classic homemade pizza with a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, and melted cheese. Customize with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Bell peppers
  • Olives
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle with cheese and add your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
